Privacy Policy

Daily Attendance

This Privacy Policy explains how the Daily Attendance app collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Daily Attendance does not collect or store your personal attendance records on our servers.

  • Attendance records are stored locally on your device.
  • Backup files are created only when you choose to export them.
  • Backup files are stored wherever you decide (for example Google Drive).

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and improve the user experience. Firebase Analytics may collect anonymous usage information such as app interactions, device type, and app version.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage information according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

The app may request storage or file access only for importing and exporting backup files selected by the user.

Data Security

Your attendance data remains under your control. We do not upload your backup files unless you manually choose to share or store them using your preferred cloud service.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
