Privacy Policy

Daily Attendance

This Privacy Policy explains how the Daily Attendance app collects, uses, stores, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Daily Attendance stores your attendance records locally on your device. Your attendance data is not uploaded to our servers. If you choose to export a backup, the backup file is saved only to the location you select.

  • Attendance records are stored securely on your device.
  • Your attendance data is not shared with us or uploaded to our servers.
  • Backup files are created only when you manually choose to export them.
  • Exported backup files are stored only in the location you choose (for example Google Drive or another storage provider).
  • If cloud synchronization or online backup features are introduced in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated before any attendance data is collected or stored on our servers.

Analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used and improve the user experience. Firebase Analytics may collect anonymous information such as app usage, device type, operating system, app version, and general interaction data.

Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and other anonymous information according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

The app may request access to files or storage only when you choose to export or import attendance backup files. The app does not access your files without your action.

Data Security

Your attendance data remains on your device unless you manually export it. We do not upload, access, or store your attendance records on our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal attendance data with third parties. Only anonymous analytics and advertising data may be processed by Google services such as Firebase Analytics and Google AdMob.

User Control

You have full control over your attendance data. You can delete your attendance records at any time by clearing the app data or uninstalling the application. You may also delete any exported backup files stored in Google Drive or any other storage location you selected.

If you believe any data associated with your use of the app needs to be removed, you may contact us at durgavatibth@gmail.com. We will respond to reasonable requests regarding data deletion in accordance with applicable laws.
